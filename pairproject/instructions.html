How to Play

---------------------------

  • The objective of the game is to shop for breakfast, lunch, and dinner!
  • Remember you only have $50 to spend, so choose your items wisely!
  • You can earn points too! Each aisle has a certain amount of points
  • For every dollar you save, half a point will be added to your scoreboard!
  • At the end of the game you can check out if you spend all of or under you budget total!
